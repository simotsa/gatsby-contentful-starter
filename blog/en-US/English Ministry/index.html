What to Expect? ...

October 29th, 2019

  • At MCAC, we hold English Worship services at 9:15AM - 10:30AM every Sunday morning. Once you step in the door, our greeters will welcome you with a smile and a bulletin with all our information. You can then feel free to step inside the sanctuary and take a couple moments to self-reflect or to stay outside and mingle for a bit before service starts.

  • We start our mornings with scripture reading and worship songs, encouraging the congregation to reflect on their weeks through both mediums. Some individuals choose to meditate in solemn silence whereas others are more vocal with their praise. We encourage any sort of honest contemplation so don't feel like you have to be a certain way when you come to MCAC! Following musical worship, there is a time of family announcements and welcome, where we share updates that the congregation needs to know and get everybody up to shake each others hands. You won't be asked to stand up if you're new but we hope that you feel welcome here and see that we want to connect with you!

  • During service, our church also practices giving through monetary offering. If you're a newcomer, please don't feel obligated to give. You can simply help pass the bags along! Our sermons are biblically based, with detailed powerpoints to follow. If you require any sort of assistance, please let the greeters know and we will do our best to accomodate you in any way.

  • Our church doesn't have a strict clothing policy. People dress on a spectrum, from full suits and dresses to jeans and a nice blouse, but most of us would fall on the casual side of the wardrobe. We ask that you dress appropriately and remember that we aren't attempting to draw attention to ourselves but to Christ.
